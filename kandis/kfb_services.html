Obtain Elgibility

BRING THE FOLLOWING ITEMS TO OBTAIN A FOOD BANK CARD:

  • Picture Identification
  • Current proof-of-address:
    • For each adult: Current mail, lease, or bill not older than 30 days
    • For each school-aged child: Current piece of mail or proof of enrollment in KSD
    • For younger children: Birth Certificate.

TO RECEIVE FULL SERVICE EACH HOUSEHOLD MUST HAVE A CURRENT FOOD BANK CARD


After receiving a food bank card bring these items at EACH visit:

  • Picture Identification
  • Current Food Bank Card
